# Sales Performance & Customer Trend Analysis

## Problem Statement
Businesses struggle to identify sales trends and high-value customers from raw data.

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle
- Records: 1000+
- Features: Date, Category, Sales, Customer ID

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Power BI
- Matplotlib

## Analysis Performed
-	Total Sales and Total Orders
-	Monthly Sales Trend
-	Sales by Product Category
-	Top Customers by Revenue

## Key Insights
-	A small number of product categories contribute a major share of total revenue
-	Sales show noticeable seasonal trends
- These insights can help businesses improve inventory planning and customer targeting
